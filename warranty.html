Warranty Policy

1.General Warranty

    1.1 Coverage: All products sold on our website are covered under this warranty policy unless explicitly stated otherwise.
    1.2 Duration: The warranty period begins from the date of purchase and lasts for 1 year.
    1.3 Eligibility: To be eligible for warranty service, proof of purchase (receipt, order confirmation) must be provided.

2.Conditions of Warranty

    2.1 Product Condition: The warranty covers products that are defective due to faulty materials or workmanship under normal use.
    2.2 Exclusions: The warranty does not cover damage caused by:
    • Misuse, abuse, or improper handling.
    • Unauthorized modifications or repairs.
    • Accidental damage, including spills, drops, and impact.
    • Normal wear and tear.
    • Software issues, including viruses and software incompatibility.

3. Warranty Claims Process

    3.1. Initiating a Claim: To initiate a warranty claim, contact our customer support team via within the warranty period.
    3.2. Information Required: Provide the following information when making a claim:
    • Proof of purchase.
    • Description of the defect.
    • Photos or videos of the defect (if applicable).

    3.3. Assessment: Our team will assess the claim and may request additional information or physical inspection of the product.

4. Remedies

    4.1. Repair: If a product is found to be defective, we will repair the product at no charge.
    4.2. Replacement: If the product cannot be repaired, we will replace it with an identical or equivalent product.
    4.3. Refund: If repair or replacement is not possible, we will issue a full or partial refund based on the product's condition and usage.

5. Additional Provisions

    5.1. Limitation of Liability: Our liability is limited to the repair, replacement, or refund of the defective product. We are not responsible for any indirect or consequential damages arising from the use of our products.
    5.2. Changes to Policy: We reserve the right to modify this warranty policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.

6. Contact Information

  • For any questions or to initiate a warranty claim, please contact our customer support team.
